What is this tool for?

This tool is designed to help ensure that your title tags and meta descriptions are the right length to remain fully visible in SERPS (Search Engine Results Pages) on all device screen sizes. This is based on the character recommendation from Moz.com. Google offers no recommendation on title tag length.

A note on meta

Google uses a number of different sources to determine what title tag and meta description is displayed. This means that the content you place within your title and description tags may not always be what appears in SERPS.
