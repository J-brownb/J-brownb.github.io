What is this tool for?

This tool is designed to help ensure that your title tags are the right length to remain fully visible in SERPS (Search Engine Results Pages) on all device screen sizes. This is based on the 50-60 character reccomendation from Moz.com. Google themselves offer no reccomendation on title tag length.

A note on title tags

Google uses a number of different sources to determine what title tag is displayed including the h1 element of the page. This means that the content you place within your title tags may not always be what appears in SERPS. You can read Google's guidelines for influencing title links here.
